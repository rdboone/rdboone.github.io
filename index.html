Environmental Informatics Using Research Infrastructures and their Data

Duffy & Ruddell, 2020

An open-source textbook that provides an introduction to environmental and ecological informatics in the context of “big science”. It provides context and hands on coding exercises for research infrastructures and observatories that collect and publish reams of observations and derived data products about the earth and its environment.
